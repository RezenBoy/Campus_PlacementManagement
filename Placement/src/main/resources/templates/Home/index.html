About Us

PlacementStudio is a platform dedicated to connecting students with top recruiters, bridging the gap between education and employment. We aim to simplify the placement process by providing a centralized system for managing campus recruitment activities. Students can explore job opportunities, submit applications, and track their progress. Recruiters can post job openings, review applications, and select candidates. We enhance communication between colleges, students, and companies for a smooth placement journey. Our goal is a transparent, efficient, and secure system.
